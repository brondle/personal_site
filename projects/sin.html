    Department of Sin Disposal is a durational performance. The performer sits, blindfolded and unable to hear, in a darkened office. Before entering, viewers write down their guilts, fears, and other things that weigh on them on a piece of paper. Upon entering, they read what they've written aloud. When they are done, they hand the paper to the performer, who eats it. It is, maybe, a place where you can be honest with another person.
