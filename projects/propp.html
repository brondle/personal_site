    A Propper Narrative is a text adventure game where the player can travel through each of Vladimir Proppp's 31 narratemes, or story functions. It's an exploration of the ways game space functions as a maps, and the ways they address that limitation to create narrative. I use the previous sentence to pitch it to serious places but it's actually a pretty goofy game.
